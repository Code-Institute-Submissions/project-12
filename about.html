About

Jezzebelle is Katie Carpenter & Gary Burke - an acoustic rhythm n' roots duo from Dublin, Ireland. Songwriters primarily, Jezzebelle have spent much of their 10yr history performing in Ireland, Europe & North America. Of their recent singles 'Meet Me In The Middle' made it onto Tunecore's 'Valentine's Day Collection' in 2013, 'Never Ends' and 'Southern Fire' (the latter co-written with Liz Madden) put Jezzebelle in the final of the prestigious 'Christie Hennessy Song Contest' in 2013 & 2014. 'Dimension Me' was a finalist in RTÃ‰'s TV song competition 'The HIT' in 2013.

'All The Stars', a reworking of Jezzebelle single 'Never Coming Back' saw them team up with Chrissy Tignor & Allied Songwriters for a brief foray into the Dance music genre in 2014. 'The Road Ahead' was the winning song in 2007's Clonmel National Song Contest, and Jezzebelle have been finalists in a number of international competitions since with tracks such as 'Empty Mile' (Glinsk 2010), 'I Just Can't Stay Away' (Clonmel 2009) and 'Turn On The Light' (ISC 2014).
